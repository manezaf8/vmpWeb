Meet Drastic Measure

Ntabethemba Mabetshe who is well known as (Maneza F8)(mc_maneza)(bhuti Maneza) in the Music scene was born In Makhaza, Khayelitsha Late 80’s and grew up moving around in the townships of Cape town, learned the street life, everyday he was always leaving his life to survive because in the streets its all about surviving, Rap music been a part of his life from the word go…

Since then he has record so many tracks most of them at SIGHT RECORDS (the home studio of Lee and PG) tracks Like “DEAR MHLOBO” that was one of the successful track in zwelethemba people would relate very much to that track because it was all about asking for forgiveness, so people would ask maneza so many questions on why did he write write the track!! And the answer was simple it was because he wanted to ask for forgiveness to his friends and tht worked!!

And lot and lot of tracks the other well known track was ZWELETHEMBA NEZIYOBISI ft Siraman , Man-bu, hard-sentence and blast!!! The track was quiet at first month of its release but as the time when on people get to knw about it and relate to the message behind it..
